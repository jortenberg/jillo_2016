Openhouser

Full Stack Web Development

Openhouser is a web application I created for real estate salespeople to manage open houses. A sign-in form generates a database of prospective clients. This database can be filtered in a number of ways to support the salesperson’s business. The app can also send automated or custom emails to any filtered list. Check out Openhouser and view the repository as well.

Bayer Aspirin

Advertising Campaign Concept

The intention, was to develop ideas where the foundation was somehow distinct to the Bayer Aspirin brand. I was most interested in the fact that Bayer Aspirin had been relieving pain since 1898. The longest continuous brand history over all other aspirin. My idea was to create stories of historical figures or moments where Bayer Aspirin helped ease the pain. While researching I learned, the word Aspirin was actually Bayer's brand name. However, Bayer's rights to the trademark were lost or sold over time. For these comps I was responsible concept, art direction, design, copy, logo, tagline, and photo research.

American Institute of Physics

Print Collateral Series

I was excited when asked to design a series of promotional brochures for the American Institute of Physics (AIP). The Brand Manager provided me with “spin-art” to use as a unifying brand element for the series. There was no budget for art, I could only use images from previously published journals. When I looked at the journals, the images were almost all charts and diagrams? The challenge was to create a look and feel for the series out of the info-graphics. There were roughly 20 different Journals published by AIP and I designed promo brochures for all of them. To the left is a sampling of some of their covers.

John Deere

Advertising Campaign Concept

I remember sitting at my desk, deadline looming, wishing an idea would come my way for John Deere equipment. Usually that is exactly the frame of mind that brings lame ideas. My best ideas generally come when I'm not trying, in places like the shower. I was looking at photographs of all kinds of John Deere machinery and started thinking about how all the machines move and transform earth. These executions followed. I was sorry the idea was not selected for production. I was responsible for concept, copy, art direction, and design.

Century 21

Advertising Campaign Concept

This idea came while visiting open houses with a friend. Our realtor commented that housing prices have nearly doubled in the past several years. There was lots of snow on the day I went out to photograph the houses, which worked out great for the art direction.
